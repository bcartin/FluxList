FluxList Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

FluxList is a task and list management app developed by Garson Tech. This policy describes what data we collect, how it is stored, and your choices regarding that data.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All FluxList data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework by default. This includes your lists, list items, projects, favorite suggestions, and usage patterns (item frequency data). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted unless you upgrade to FluxList Pro and sign in with an account.

Data Synced to the Cloud (Pro Users)

If you purchase FluxList Pro and create an account, the following data is synced to Google Firebase (Firestore) to enable cloud backup and list sharing:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and Firebase authentication identifier.
  • Lists and items: List names, item names, completion status, color and icon preferences, creation dates, and which users have access to each list.
  • Projects: Project names.
  • Favorites and usage data: Your favorite item suggestions and item frequency counts (used to generate smart suggestions).
  • Friends: The identifiers of users you have added as friends.

This data is associated with your Firebase account and stored on Google's servers. See Google's privacy policy for information about how Google handles data on its infrastructure.

Authentication

FluxList uses Firebase Authentication for account management. When you sign up or sign in, your email address and password are handled by Firebase. FluxList does not store your password.

In-App Purchases

FluxList Pro is available as an in-app purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. Apple's purchase verification system is used to confirm your subscription status.

Analytics and Tracking

FluxList does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks. We do not track your activity or share your data with advertisers.

Data Sharing

Your data is not sold to third parties. Data is only shared with other FluxList users when you explicitly share a list with them. Shared list collaborators can see the list name, items, and completion status of lists you share.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local data can be deleted at any time by deleting the app from your device. If you are a Pro user with cloud sync enabled, you can delete your account and associated cloud data by contacting us. Upon account deletion, all data stored in Firebase will be removed.

Children's Privacy

FluxList is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on our website.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at garsontech@gmail.com.
